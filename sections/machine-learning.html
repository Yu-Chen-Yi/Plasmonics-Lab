Combining images captured by meta-lenses with artificial intelligence models based on convolutional neural networks can enable meta-lenses to deliver higher-quality images. For instance, GaN (gallium nitride) meta-lenses may produce images with a reddish color cast due to blue light losses inherent to the material, and the image details may also lack clarity.
