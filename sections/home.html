🌌 Welcome to Nano Optics Laboratory

🔬 奈米光學實驗室致力於探索前沿的光學技術與創新研究，專注於超穎介面、光學設計及奈米技術的前瞻性應用。

🔬 The Nano Optics Laboratory is dedicated to exploring cutting-edge optical technologies and innovative research, focusing on metasurfaces, optical design, and advanced nanotechnology applications.


🎯 我們的使命

透過創新的研究與教育，培養新一代的光學與奈米技術專家，並推動學術界與產業界的進步。

🎯 Our Mission

To advance innovation in optics and nanotechnology, fostering the next generation of experts through research and education, while contributing to both academic and industrial progress.
