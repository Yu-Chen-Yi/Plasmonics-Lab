🔬 我們的實驗室誠徵對於超穎介面與光學設計感興趣的同學加入研究團隊，共同探索前沿光學與奈米技術領域。

🔬 Our laboratory is seeking highly motivated students interested in metasurfaces and optical design to join our research team and explore cutting-edge topics in optics and nanotechnology.

📚 研究主題與訓練

我們的實驗室目前主要工作集中在利用超穎介面做影像相關的研究主題。學生將從 幾何光學光束追跡 開始學習光學設計，進一步探索 次波長尺度天線 與 CMOS 製程技術。

📚 Research Topics and Training

Our research primarily focuses on metasurface technologies for imaging applications. Students will start with geometrical optics ray tracing and progress towards designing sub-wavelength antennas and working with CMOS fabrication techniques.

🛠️ 技能訓練

程式方面: Python、MATLAB

設計及模擬方面: 幾何光學光束追跡、電磁波計算 (FDTD, RCWA)、波動光學計算 (BPM)

製程方面: 電子束微影、奈米壓印技術、一般半導體製程技術

量測方面: 光學桌技能與量測系統設計與搭建

🛠️ Skills Acquired

Programming: Python, MATLAB

Design and Simulation: Geometrical optics ray tracing, Electromagnetic simulations (FDTD, RCWA), Wave optics modeling (BPM)

Fabrication: E-beam lithography, Nanoimprint Lithography, Standard semiconductor fabrication techniques

Measurement: Optical table operation and measurement system setup
